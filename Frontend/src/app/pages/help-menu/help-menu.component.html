General Questions

ECHO is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that recommends music based entirely on your personal listening profile. It also displays visualisations of statistics based on the analysis of the music you love. It uses cutting-edge AI to explore abstract song characteristics, such as theme and mood, helping you better understand your listening habits and discover new favourites.

To get started, simply create an account or sign in using an existing music provider like Spotify, YouTube, or Deezer. Once connected, ECHO will analyze your listening history and provide personalized recommendations and insightful visualizations.

ECHO is a Progressive Web App, which means you can access it on most modern web browsers, including those on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. You can also install it directly from your browser onto your device for a native app-like experience.

Features & Functionalities

ECHO uses AI to analyze your listening habits and generate recommendations based on the emotional tone, theme, and characteristics of the music you enjoy. This includes analyzing elements like rhythm, mood, and even the context in which you often listen to certain songs.

ECHO provides dynamic visualizations that show your mood trends, genre preferences, most-listened themes, and listening patterns over time. These insights help you explore your music habits in a more interactive and visually engaging way.

Yes! Since ECHO is a Progressive Web App, it offers offline functionality. You can access previously loaded visualizations and music insights even when you are not connected to the internet.

ECHO's AI analyzes various musical characteristics like tempo, key, rhythm, and lyrical content to assess the overall mood of each song. The result is a categorized list of moods that help recommend tracks based on your current emotional state.

Personalization & Customization

Absolutely! ECHO allows you to customize the interface to suit your preferences, including selecting color themes, enabling dark mode, adjusting font sizes, and more. These features ensure the app is accessible and comfortable for all users.

To connect other music services, go to the settings menu and select “Connect Music Providers.” From there, you can link your Spotify, YouTube, or Deezer account, allowing ECHO to analyze your listening data across multiple platforms.

Yes, ECHO allows you to generate AI-crafted playlists based on mood, activity, or themes. Simply select your desired mood or activity, and ECHO will create a playlist tailored to your preferences, which you can save to your connected music service.

Technical & Troubleshooting

To install ECHO, open it in your browser and click on the “Install App” button in the address bar (available in most modern browsers like Chrome or Edge). This will add an icon to your home screen or desktop, allowing for easy access.

If visualizations aren’t loading, make sure you’re connected to the internet and have allowed ECHO to access your listening history. If you’re offline, previously generated visualizations will still be available, but new insights may require a connection.

Ensure you’re logged into your connected music provider and have granted ECHO permission to access your data. You may also need to check your internet connection or refresh the app if playback issues persist.

Privacy & Security

Yes, your privacy is a top priority. ECHO uses encryption to protect your data, both in transit and at rest. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with personalized recommendations and visualizations. You can manage and review data permissions in the app’s settings.

ECHO does not permanently store your music history. We use your listening data temporarily to generate insights and visualizations, but this information is anonymized and securely processed to maintain your privacy.

To delete your data, go to the settings menu and select “Delete Account and Data.” This will remove all your personal information and history from our system.

Advanced Features

The Mood Room feature allows you to create a shared music experience with friends. You can invite friends to join a Mood Room, and ECHO will generate a shared playlist based on everyone’s current mood, making it perfect for group gatherings or shared listening sessions.

ECHO uses a combination of machine learning and natural language processing algorithms to understand various song attributes, including lyrical content, instrumental arrangement, and emotional undertones. These analyses help to recommend music and generate visual insights about your preferences.

To enable push notifications, go to the settings menu and turn on notifications. You’ll receive updates about new song recommendations, insights into your music habits, and special features like new playlist suggestions.
